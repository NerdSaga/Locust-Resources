Account access

Get help signing in

Try these steps if your email or password is not being accepted.

Instructions

  1. Check that you are using the email address connected to your volunteer account.
  2. Check for extra spaces and confirm that Caps Lock is off.
  3. Select Forgot password? on the sign-in screen.
  4. Open the reset email and follow its secure link.
  5. If the email does not arrive, check your spam folder before contacting support.

Security tip: Support should never ask you to share your password.
